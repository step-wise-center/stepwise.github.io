About Step Wise

Welcome to Step Wise. We are a leading health and wellness center designed to enhance the quality of life for individuals living with dementia and their families, combining expert care with the comfort of a hotel. Our mission is to provide our guests with an environment where they feel safe, understood, and valued by combining clinical expertise with a homelike design. We integrate innovative treatment methods such as olfactory tunnels, sensory workshops, and TPS/DCS into personalized care plans.

THERAPIES AND FACILITIES

Our center offers holistic services designed to support both the mental and physical well-being of our guests. Our expert team, with personalized therapy, activity, and care programs tailored to each individual's needs, aims to maximize their quality of life.

Sensory Workshops

Stimulating the senses and motor skills through creative activities such as art, music, and ceramics.

Cognitive Therapy

Supporting cognitive functions with modern methods such as TPS/DCS.

Scent Tunnel & Aromatherapy

Stimulating olfactory memory and increasing sensory awareness.

24/7 Expert Care & Support

Professional health monitoring to ensure guests feel safe and supported at all times.

Secure & Accessible Architecture

Special design that facilitates circulation and provides a safe and "home" feeling.

Personalized Nutrition

Healthy menus planned by expert dietitians that support cognitive health.
